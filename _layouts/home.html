Welcome to my personal website! I am a Ph.D. candidate at the dynamic DYROS LAB at Seoul National University (SNU). My research is dedicated to improving the safety and efficiency of human labor through robotics. I focus on applying machine learning to humanoid robots, enhancing their capabilities in collision handling, agile locomotion, and dexterous manipulation.

- B.A in Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering. Mar. 2012 - Feb. 2017. Seoul National University, Seoul, Republic of Korea
